# Water Level Indicator

## Description

The Water Level Indicator is an electronic system used to monitor the water level inside a tank. It uses sensors placed at different levels of the tank to detect the presence of water and indicate the level using LEDs.

This system helps prevent water overflow and dry running of pumps by providing a visual indication of the tank's water level.

## Components Used

* Arduino UNO (optional for advanced version)
* Water Level Sensors / Probes
* LEDs
* Resistors
* Connecting Wires
* Power Supply

## Working Principle

Sensors or probes are placed at different heights inside the water tank.

When water reaches a specific level:

* The corresponding sensor detects the water
* Arduino processes the signal
* The respective LED turns ON

Each LED represents a different water level such as Low, Medium, and Full.

## Applications

* Home water tank monitoring
* Industrial water storage systems
* Water management systems
* Pump protection systems
